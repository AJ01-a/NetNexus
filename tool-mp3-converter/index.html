▲ ON YOUTUBE / STREAMING LINKS

This bay deliberately does not rip audio from YouTube or other streaming URLs. Pulling media off those platforms breaks their Terms of Service and usually the source's copyright, and the public "conversion endpoints" that claim to do it are unreliable, ad-laden, and frequently malicious. So I have left that door shut, sir — on principle and for your safety.

For audio you legitimately own or have created, this tool is fully functional: drop the file above to trim, adjust gain, and re-export locally. To pull the audio track from a video you own, the clean route is the open-source yt-dlp (for your own uploads) or ffmpeg -i in.mp4 -q:a 0 out.mp3 on your own machine.
